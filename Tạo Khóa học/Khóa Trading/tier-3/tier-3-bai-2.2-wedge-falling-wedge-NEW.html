üìö Falling Wedge La Gi?

Falling Wedge la mot trong nhung bullish reversal pattern duoc trader ua chuong nhat. Pattern nay xuat hien khi gia giam dan nhung luc ban yeu di. No bao hieu xu huong giam sap ket thuc va gia se tang manh.

Day la nguoc lai hoan toan voi Rising Wedge ma ban hoc o bai truoc. Neu Rising Wedge la bearish thi Falling Wedge la bullish. Hieu ca hai se giup ban trade hieu qua hon.

üéØ Dinh nghia chinh xac

Falling Wedge la pattern voi hai duong trendline hoi tu huong xuong. Duong resistance noi cac dinh thap dan. Duong support noi cac day thap dan. Khoang cach giua hai duong thu hep lai theo thoi gian.

Hinh 2.2.1: Cau truc co ban cua Falling Wedge - Bullish Reversal Pattern

üîß Cau Truc Cua Falling Wedge

Falling Wedge co cau truc nguoc lai voi Rising Wedge. Hieu ro tung thanh phan giup ban nhan dien pattern chinh xac hon nhieu.

üìâ
1. Downtrend Ban Dau
Falling Wedge thuong hinh thanh sau mot giai doan giam gia. Day la dieu kien de pattern mang y nghia reversal. Gia phai giam truoc do thi breakout moi co gia tri.
üìê
2. Hai Duong Trendline Hoi Tu
Duong tren noi cac dinh Lower High. Duong duoi noi cac day Lower Low. Ca hai duong deu huong xuong nhung duong tren doc hon. Dieu nay tao hinh nem thu hep huong xuong.
üìä
3. Volume Giam Dan
Volume thuong giam dan khi pattern phat trien. Day la dau hieu cho thay luc ban dang yeu di. Nguoi ban mat dan dong luc tiep tuc ban. Day la tin hieu bullish tiem an.
üöÄ
4. Breakout
Gia pha vo duong resistance phia tren voi volume tang manh. Day la tin hieu vao lenh Long. Breakout thuong xay ra o khoang 2/3 chieu dai cua wedge.
üí° Luu Y Quan Trong

Falling Wedge can it nhat 2 diem cham cho moi duong trendline. Ly tuong la 3 diem cham tro len. Mot so trader doi 5 diem cham de dam bao pattern that su dang tin cay.

üß† Tam Ly Dang Sau Falling Wedge

Falling Wedge phan anh su thay doi tam ly tu bi quan sang lac quan. Hieu dieu nay giup ban tu tin hon khi trade pattern nay.

üò∞ Giai Doan 1: Bi Quan

Nguoi ban van bi quan va tiep tuc day gia xuong. Ho tin rang xu huong giam se tiep tuc. Nhung moi dot giam gia lai yeu hon lan truoc.

ü§î Giai Doan 2: Suy Nghi Lai

Nguoi ban bat dau suy nghi lai khi gia giam cham lai. Mot so nguoi mua bat dau tich luy. Thi truong dang can bang lai dan dan.

üòä Giai Doan 3: Ky Vong

Nguoi mua bat dau tin tuong hon. Ho thay luc ban yeu di ro rang. Smart money bat dau tich luy o vung thap. Day la giai doan chuan bi.

üéâ Giai Doan 4: Bung No

Khi breakout xay ra, nguoi mua nhay vao manh me. Nguoi Short bi quet stop loss. Gia tang manh va nhanh. Day la luc profit lon nhat.

üéØ Key Insight

Falling Wedge the hien su kiet suc cua phe ban. Gia giam nhung volume giam cho thay it nguoi muon ban o muc gia thap. Khi khong con ai ban, gia chi co the tang.

üìä So Sanh Rising Wedge vs Falling Wedge

Hieu su khac biet giua hai pattern giup ban tranh nham lan. Day la bang so sanh chi tiet va de nho.

Dac Diem Rising Wedge Falling Wedge
Huong Pattern Huong len Huong xuong
Loai Pattern Bearish Reversal Bullish Reversal
Breakout Xuong duoi (Breakdown) Len tren (Breakout)
Win Rate 67-72% 68-74%
Entry Short sau breakdown Long sau breakout
Stop Loss Tren dinh wedge Duoi day wedge

Hinh 2.2.2: So sanh truc quan giua Rising Wedge va Falling Wedge

üìà Cach Trade Falling Wedge Hieu Qua

Day la quy trinh trade Falling Wedge ma cac trader chuyen nghiep su dung. Lam theo tung buoc de tang ty le thanh cong.

üéØ Entry Strategy
  • Conservative: Cho gia dong cua tren resistance + retest thanh cong
  • Aggressive: Vao lenh ngay khi gia break len tren resistance
  • Confirmation: Volume phai tang it nhat 50% so voi trung binh
üìè Price Target
  • Target 1: Chieu cao cua wedge (tu dinh den day) tinh tu diem breakout
  • Target 2: Dinh cua downtrend truoc do (neu co)
  • Target 3: Fibonacci 61.8% retracement cua downtrend truoc do
üõë Stop Loss
  • Vi tri: Dat stop loss duoi day thap nhat cua wedge
  • Buffer: Them 1-2% de tranh bi quet boi noise
  • Risk/Reward: Dam bao ty le toi thieu 1:2 truoc khi vao lenh
üí° Pro Tip

Falling Wedge co win rate cao hon Rising Wedge mot chut (68-74% vs 67-72%). Ly do la breakout bullish thuong manh hon vi thuong co short squeeze kem theo.

‚ö†Ô∏è Canh Bao Va Luu Y

Khong phai Falling Wedge nao cung thanh cong. Day la nhung dieu ban can luu y de tranh mat tien.

‚è∞ Thoi Gian Hinh Thanh

Wedge can thoi gian de phat trien. Pattern qua ngan (duoi 3 nen) thuong khong dang tin cay. Ly tuong la 10-50 nen.

üìä Volume Confirmation

Volume giam trong wedge, tang manh khi breakout. Neu breakout khong co volume, do la false breakout.

üîÑ Retest

Sau breakout, gia thuong quay lai test resistance cu. Day la co hoi entry tot nhat neu ban bo lo lan dau.

üìà Trend Context

Falling Wedge trong uptrend lon la continuation. Falling Wedge trong downtrend la reversal. Boi canh rat quan trong.

üìã Tom Tat Bai Hoc
  • Falling Wedge la bullish reversal pattern voi win rate 68-74%
  • Hai duong trendline hoi tu huong xuong, khoang cach thu hep dan
  • Volume giam dan la dau hieu xac nhan quan trong
  • Entry khi gia break len resistance voi volume tang
  • Target bang chieu cao wedge tinh tu diem breakout
  • Stop loss dat duoi day thap nhat cua wedge
