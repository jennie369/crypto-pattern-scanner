‚ùì Tai Sao Can Phan Biet?

Wedge va Triangle la hai nhom pattern de bi nham lan nhat trong technical analysis. Ca hai deu co hai duong trendline hoi tu. Ca hai deu cho thay gia dang bi nen lai. Nhung chung co y nghia hoan toan khac nhau.

Nham lan giua hai pattern nay co the khien ban vao lenh sai huong. Ban co the Long khi nen Short hoac nguoc lai. Hieu ro su khac biet se giup ban trade chinh xac hon nhieu.

‚ö†Ô∏è Loi Pho Bien

Nhieu trader nham Ascending Triangle voi Rising Wedge vi ca hai deu huong len. Nhung Ascending Triangle la bullish, con Rising Wedge la bearish. Day la loi nghiem trong can tranh.

Hinh 2.3.1: Tong quan su khac biet giua Wedge va Triangle patterns

üìê Dac Diem Cua Wedge Patterns

Wedge co nhung dac diem rieng biet giup ban nhan ra no ngay lap tuc. Nho nhung dac diem nay se giup ban phan biet nhanh hon.

üìê
Ca Hai Duong Cung Huong
Trong Wedge, ca hai duong trendline deu nghieng cung mot huong. Rising Wedge co ca hai duong huong len. Falling Wedge co ca hai duong huong xuong. Day la dac diem quan trong nhat.
üîÑ
Reversal Pattern
Wedge thuong la reversal pattern. Rising Wedge bao hieu bearish reversal. Falling Wedge bao hieu bullish reversal. Huong cua pattern nguoc voi huong breakout du kien.
üìä
Win Rate Cao
Wedge co win rate kha cao tu 67-74%. Day la mot trong nhung pattern dang tin cay nhat. Ly do la vi no phan anh su kiet suc cua trend hien tai.
üéØ Cach Nho Nhanh

Wedge giong nhu cai nem - thu hep ve mot huong. Ca hai canh cua nem deu huong cung mot phia. Day la hinh anh de nho nhat.

üî∫ Dac Diem Cua Triangle Patterns

Triangle co 3 loai chinh: Symmetric, Ascending va Descending. Moi loai co dac diem va y nghia rieng.

1. Symmetric Triangle

Duong tren nghieng xuong, duong duoi nghieng len. Hai duong gap nhau o giua. Pattern trung lap, co the break ca hai huong.

  • Win Rate: 54-57% (phu thuoc huong break)
  • Thuong la continuation pattern
2. Ascending Triangle

Duong tren ngang (resistance), duong duoi nghieng len. Pattern bullish, thuong break len tren.

  • Win Rate: 72-75% khi breakout len
  • Cho thay nguoi mua dang manh hon
3. Descending Triangle

Duong tren nghieng xuong, duong duoi ngang (support). Pattern bearish, thuong break xuong duoi.

  • Win Rate: 64-68% khi breakdown
  • Cho thay nguoi ban dang manh hon

Hinh 2.3.2: Ba loai Triangle Pattern co ban

üìä Bang So Sanh Chi Tiet

Day la bang so sanh day du giup ban phan biet Wedge va Triangle mot cach nhanh chong va chinh xac.

Dac Diem Wedge Triangle
Huong 2 duong Cung huong (ca 2 len hoac ca 2 xuong) Khac huong hoac 1 ngang
Loai Pattern Thuong la Reversal Thuong la Continuation
Huong Breakout Nguoc huong pattern Cung huong trend truoc
Win Rate 67-74% 54-75% (tuy loai)
Thoi Gian Thuong ngan hon Co the dai hon
Volume Giam dan ro rang Giam dan it hon
üí° Quy Tac Vang

Neu ca hai duong trendline cung nghieng ve mot phia - do la Wedge. Neu mot duong ngang hoac hai duong nghieng nguoc nhau - do la Triangle.

üîç Phan Biet Chi Tiet Tung Cap

Hay xem ky tung cap pattern de bi nham lan nhat. Hieu ro su khac biet giup ban khong bao gio nham lan nua.

Rising Wedge vs Ascending Triangle

Rising Wedge: Ca 2 duong nghieng len, bearish
Ascending Triangle: Duong tren ngang, duong duoi len, bullish

Falling Wedge vs Descending Triangle

Falling Wedge: Ca 2 duong nghieng xuong, bullish
Descending Triangle: Duong duoi ngang, duong tren xuong, bearish

üéØ Meo Nho Nhanh
  • Wedge: "Nem" - ca hai canh cung huong nhu cai nem
  • Triangle: "Tam giac" - co it nhat mot canh ngang hoac hai canh nguoc nhau
  • Breakout: Wedge break nguoc huong pattern, Triangle break cung huong trend

Hinh 2.3.3: So sanh truc quan Wedge va Triangle canh nhau

üìà Chien Luoc Trade Cho Tung Loai

Moi loai pattern can chien luoc trade khac nhau. Hieu dieu nay giup ban toi uu hoa profit.

üìê Chien Luoc Trade Wedge
  • Cho breakout nguoc huong pattern
  • Volume phai tang manh khi breakout
  • Target = Chieu cao wedge tu diem breakout
  • Stop loss o phia ben kia cua pattern
üî∫ Chien Luoc Trade Triangle
  • Cho breakout cung huong trend truoc (thuong la)
  • Ascending: mua khi break len resistance
  • Descending: ban khi break xuong support
  • Symmetric: cho confirmation huong break
üìä So Sanh Win Rate
  • Rising Wedge Breakdown: 67-72%
  • Falling Wedge Breakout: 68-74%
  • Ascending Triangle Breakout: 72-75%
  • Descending Triangle Breakdown: 64-68%
  • Symmetric Triangle: 54-57%

‚ö†Ô∏è Nhung Truong Hop Kho Phan Biet

Trong thuc te, mot so truong hop kho phan biet giua Wedge va Triangle. Day la cach xu ly nhung tinh huong do.

Khi Khong Chac Chan

Neu khong chac la Wedge hay Triangle, cho breakout xay ra. Huong breakout se giup xac nhan loai pattern.

Kiem Tra Volume

Wedge co volume giam ro rang hon Triangle. Neu volume giam manh, do nhieu kha nang la Wedge.

Xem Boi Canh

Xem trend truoc do. Pattern trong uptrend manh khong nen la Ascending Triangle ma la Rising Wedge.

Do Goc Nghieng

Wedge thuong co goc hoi tu lon hon. Triangle thuong rong hon va gap nhau xa hon.

üìã Tom Tat Bai Hoc
  • Wedge co ca 2 duong cung huong, Triangle co duong khac huong hoac 1 ngang
  • Wedge la reversal, Triangle thuong la continuation
  • Wedge break nguoc huong pattern, Triangle break cung huong trend
  • Rising Wedge (bearish) khac Ascending Triangle (bullish)
  • Falling Wedge (bullish) khac Descending Triangle (bearish)
  • Khi khong chac, cho breakout xac nhan truoc khi vao lenh
