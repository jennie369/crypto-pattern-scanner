Thanh Cong

Bitcoin hinh thanh Rising Wedge tren chart 4H sau dot tang manh tu $40,000 len $48,000. Pattern phat trien trong 12 ngay truoc khi breakdown xay ra.

  • Dat stop loss tren dinh wedge de bao ve von
  • Risk/Reward ratio 1:5 la ly tuong cho trade nay

Case Study 3: Falling Wedge tren SOL/USDT
Thanh Cong

Solana hinh thanh Falling Wedge hoan hao tren chart Daily sau dot giam 40%. Pattern phat trien trong 3 tuan truoc khi breakout mang lai profit lon.

Tuan 3

üìù Tai Sao Trade Nay Thanh Cong
  • Pattern hoan hao voi 5 diem cham moi duong trendline
  • Volume giam dan deu - xac nhan pattern rat tot
  • RSI divergence ho tro - them confirmation
  • Entry sau retest - vi tri an toan hon
  • Risk/Reward ratio 1:4.5 rat hap dan

üìä Tong Ket 3 Case Studies

Sau khi phan tich 3 case study, day la nhung diem quan trong ban can nho. Hoc tu ca thanh cong lan that bai de tro thanh trader tot hon.

Dieu Lam Dung

Cho confirmation truoc khi vao lenh. Kiem tra volume can than. Dat stop loss hop ly. Cho retest de co entry tot hon.

Dieu Lam Sai

Vao lenh truoc khi breakdown/breakout. Bo qua volume signal. Khong xem xet boi canh lon. Tham lam khong chot loi.

üìà Thong Ke Tu 3 Trades
  • Ty le thanh cong: 2/3 (67%)
  • Tong profit: +34.3% (7.4% + 31.6% - 4.7%)
  • Average win: +19.5%
  • Average loss: -4.7%
  • Win/Loss ratio: 4.1 - Rat tot!
