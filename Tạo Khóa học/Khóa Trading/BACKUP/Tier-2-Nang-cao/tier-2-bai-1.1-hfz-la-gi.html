üìç Dinh Nghia HFZ

HFZ

High Frequency Zone
= Vung co nhieu lenh BAN cho khop
Khi gia quay lai vung nay, co xac suat cao gia se GIAM

Dac diem cua HFZ:

  • Vi tri: Luon nam TREN gia hien tai
  • Tin hieu: SHORT (ban) khi gia quay lai kiem tra
  • Nguon goc: Duoc tao tu patterns bearish
  • Mau sac: Thuong danh dau bang mau DO

üîÑ HFZ Duoc Tao Tu Patterns Nao?

HFZ duoc hinh thanh tu 2 patterns chinh trong he thong GEM Frequency:

DPD
Down ‚Üí Pause ‚Üí Down
UPD
Up ‚Üí Pause ‚Üí Down

Tai sao nhung patterns nay tao HFZ?

Khi gia tao pattern DPD hoac UPD:

  1. Phase 1: Gia di chuyen (Up hoac Down)
  2. Phase 2: Gia PAUSE (tich luy) - Day la vung HFZ!
  3. Phase 3: Gia pha xuong (Down)
üí° Ghi nho:

Vung Pause trong DPD va UPD chua nhieu lenh BAN cua institutional traders. Khi gia quay lai, ho se tiep tuc ban, tao ap luc giam gia.

‚ö° Cach Su Dung HFZ Trong Trading

Gia cham HFZ = TIN HIEU SHORT (BAN)
Cho gia quay lai vung HFZ, tim xac nhan rejection, va vao lenh SHORT

Quy trinh trading voi HFZ:

  1. Xac dinh HFZ: Tim vung Pause cua DPD hoac UPD
  2. Doi gia quay lai: Gia phai cham vao vung HFZ
  3. Tim xac nhan: Nen rejection (Pin bar, Engulfing...)
  4. Entry SHORT: Sau khi co xac nhan bearish
  5. Stop Loss: Tren dinh cua HFZ zone

üü¢ LFZ (Buy Zone)
