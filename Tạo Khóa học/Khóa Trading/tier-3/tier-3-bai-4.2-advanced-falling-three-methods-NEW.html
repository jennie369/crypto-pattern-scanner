üìö Falling Three Methods La Gi?

Falling Three Methods la mot candlestick pattern co nguon goc tu Nhat Ban. Pattern nay xuat hien trong xu huong giam va bao hieu gia se tiep tuc giam. No thuoc nhom continuation pattern (mau hinh tiep dien).

Pattern nay gom 5 cay nen lien tiep. Nen dau va nen cuoi la nen giam lon. Ba nen giua la nen tang nho nam trong pham vi nen dau. Day la dau hieu cua su nghi ngoi truoc khi giam tiep.

üìñ Dinh Nghia Chinh Xac

Falling Three Methods la bearish continuation pattern gom 5 cay nen. Mot nen giam lon, ba nen tang nho, va mot nen giam lon pha xuong. Pattern xac nhan xu huong giam se tiep tuc.

Hinh 4.2.1: Cau truc 5 cay nen cua Falling Three Methods

üìä Dieu Kien Xac Nhan Pattern

Khong phai moi nhom 5 nen deu la Falling Three Methods hop le. Ban can kiem tra nhieu dieu kien de xac nhan. Duoi day la cac tieu chuan bat buoc.

üìâ Xu Huong Truoc

Phai co xu huong giam ro rang truoc khi pattern xuat hien. Pattern nay chi hop le trong downtrend.

üìè Nen Dau Va Cuoi

Ca hai nen phai la bearish (do) voi than dai. Nen cuoi dong cua duoi day nen dau la ly tuong.

üîÑ Ba Nen Giua

Thuong la nen bullish (xanh) nho. Khong duoc vuot qua high cua nen dau hoac duoi low cua nen dau.

üìâ Volume

Volume cao o nen dau, giam dan o 3 nen giua, tang lai o nen cuoi. Day la dau hieu xac nhan.

‚ö†Ô∏è Canh Bao Quan Trong

Neu ba nen giua vuot qua high cua nen dau, pattern bi vo hieu. Khi do, xu huong co the dao chieu thay vi tiep tuc giam. Hay can than quan sat.

‚ö° Tam Ly Thi Truong

Hieu tam ly dang sau pattern giup ban trade tu tin hon. Falling Three Methods phan anh cuoc chien giua Buyer va Seller. Day la cau chuyen cua su thua nhan va tiep tuc.

üß† Tam Ly Tung Giai Doan
  • Nen 1: Seller thong tri, day gia xuong manh. Buyer hoang so.
  • Nen 2-4: Buyer co gang hoi phuc nhung thieu luc. Ho chi mua nho giot.
  • Nen 5: Seller nhan ra Buyer yeu, tiep tuc tan cong manh.

Ba nen tang nho giua la giai doan "nghi ngoi" cua Seller. Ho khong rut lui, chi tam dung de tich luy them. Khi Buyer can suc, ho quay lai manh me hon.

üí° Key Insight

Falling Three Methods khong phai la reversal that bai. Day la continuation pattern. Ba nen tang giua la co hoi de Seller vao them lenh Short voi gia tot hon.

üéØ Cach Trade Falling Three Methods

Khi da nhan dien pattern chinh xac, ban can co ke hoach trade cu the. Duoi day la cac buoc thuc hien va quan ly vi the. Lam theo de tang ty le thanh cong.

üìê Ke Hoach Vao Lenh
  • Entry: Vao Short khi nen 5 dong cua, hoac doi pullback nhe
  • Stop Loss: Dat tren high cua nen 1 (hoac high cao nhat cua 3 nen giua)
  • Take Profit: Muc tieu bang chieu dai nen 1 tinh tu day nen 1

‚úÖ Nen Lam

‚ùå Khong Nen Lam

Vao lenh khi nen 5 chua dong. Trade khi volume khong xac nhan. Dat SL qua sat entry.

üìà Cac Loi Thuong Gap

Nhieu trader mac loi khi trade Falling Three Methods. Hoc tu sai lam cua nguoi khac giup ban tranh duoc ton that. Duoi day la cac loi pho bien nhat.

‚ö†Ô∏è Loi So 1: Vao Lenh Som

Nhieu nguoi vao Short khi moi thay nen 4 hoac nen 5 dang hinh thanh. Pattern chua hoan chinh nen rui ro cao. Luon cho nen 5 dong cua truoc khi trade.

‚ö†Ô∏è Loi So 2: Bo Qua Context

Pattern xuat hien tai vung ho tro manh co the that bai. Luon kiem tra co vung Support hoac tin tuc quan trong nao khong truoc khi vao lenh.

‚ö†Ô∏è Loi So 3: Khong Quan Ly Risk

Dat Stop Loss qua xa hoac qua gan deu sai. SL tren high cua pattern la hop ly. Dung bao gio trade khong co SL.
