## GENERAL IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### Technical Requirements
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9) or 1080x1920 (9:16) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Format: PNG with transparency where applicable
- Color profile: sRGB
- Quality: High (no compression artifacts)

### Style Consistency
- All images should feel cohesive and part of same course
- Dark theme throughout with strategic light accents
- Vietnamese text should be clear and readable
- Glassmorphism effects on cards/overlays
- Cosmic/spiritual undertones
- Professional, premium quality feel

### File Naming Convention
- HINH_[DAY]_[NUMBER]_[DESCRIPTION].png
- Example: HINH_3_2_NGUYEN_LY_KHAM_NHAN.png
