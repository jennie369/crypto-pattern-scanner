üìö Cup and Handle La Gi?

Cup and Handle la mot trong nhung pattern manh nhat trong phan tich ky thuat. Pattern nay duoc phat hien boi William O'Neil vao thap nien 1980. No bao hieu su tiep tuc cua xu huong tang voi do tin cay cao.

Pattern nay trong giong nhu mot chiec coc co tay cam. Phan coc la giai doan tich luy hinh chu U. Phan tay cam la giai doan dieu chinh nhe truoc khi gia bung no.

üìñ Dinh Nghia Chinh Xac

Cup and Handle la continuation pattern (mau hinh tiep dien) xuat hien sau mot xu huong tang. Gia tao thanh hinh chu U (Cup), sau do dieu chinh nhe (Handle), roi breakout manh len tren.

65-70%
Win Rate trung binh khi trading Cup and Handle dung chuan

Cup and Handle la pattern yeu thich cua cac trader chuyen nghiep. No cho ty le thanh cong cao va muc tieu loi nhuan ro rang. Ban se hoc cach nhan dien va trade pattern nay hieu qua.

üîß Cau Truc Cua Cup and Handle

Cup and Handle gom 4 giai doan chinh. Moi giai doan co dac diem rieng ve gia va volume. Hieu ro tung giai doan giup ban trade chinh xac hon.

üìà
1. Xu Huong Tang Truoc (Prior Uptrend)
Gia phai co xu huong tang truoc khi Cup hinh thanh. Dieu nay xac nhan day la continuation pattern. Xu huong tang it nhat 30% la ly tuong.
‚òï
2. Cup (Phan Coc)
Gia giam dan tao thanh day chu U. Day Cup khong nen qua sau (toi da 30-35% tu dinh). Thoi gian hinh thanh Cup tu 7 tuan den 65 tuan.
üîß
3. Handle (Tay Cam)
Sau khi gia hoi phuc len gan dinh cu, no dieu chinh nhe. Handle giam 10-15% tu dinh Cup. Thoi gian Handle tu 1-4 tuan la ly tuong.
üöÄ
4. Breakout
Gia pha vo duong khang cu cua Handle. Volume tang manh khi breakout xay ra. Day la diem vao lenh ly tuong cho trader.

Hinh 4.1.1: Cau truc 4 giai doan cua Cup and Handle Pattern

üìä Dieu Kien Nhan Dien Chuan

Khong phai moi hinh dang giong Cup and Handle deu la pattern that. Ban can kiem tra nhieu dieu kien de xac nhan pattern hop le. Duoi day la cac tieu chuan quan trong.

üìè Do Sau Cua Cup

Day Cup khong nen qua 30-35% tu dinh. Neu Cup qua sau, pattern yeu di. Do sau ly tuong la 15-25% tu dinh.

‚è±Ô∏è Thoi Gian

Cup can it nhat 7 tuan de hinh thanh. Handle can 1-4 tuan. Tong thoi gian tu 2-6 thang la ly tuong.

üìâ Volume Pattern

Volume giam dan khi Cup hinh thanh. Volume tang manh khi breakout. Day la dau hieu xac nhan quan trong.

üîß Handle Position

Handle phai nam o nua tren cua Cup. Neu Handle qua thap, pattern mat hieu luc. Vi tri ly tuong la 1/3 tren cua Cup.

‚ö†Ô∏è Luu Y Quan Trong

Hinh dang chu U tot hon chu V. Cup co day nhat giup gia on dinh hon. Day chu V thuong la dau hieu cua su hoi phuc khong ben vung.

üéØ Cach Tinh Price Target

Cup and Handle co cach tinh muc tieu gia ro rang. Cong thuc nay giup ban dat Take Profit hop ly. No cung giup danh gia Risk/Reward truoc khi vao lenh.

üìê Cong Thuc Tinh Target
  • Buoc 1: Do chieu sau cua Cup (tu dinh den day)
  • Buoc 2: Xac dinh diem Breakout (dinh cua Handle)
  • Buoc 3: Target = Breakout + Chieu sau Cup

Vi du: Neu dinh Cup la $100, day Cup la $70, chieu sau la $30. Khi gia breakout tai $100, target la $130. Day la muc tieu toi thieu, gia co the tang hon.

83%
Ty le dat duoc target khi pattern hoan chinh dung chuan

‚ö° Tam Ly Thi Truong

Hieu tam ly dang sau Cup and Handle giup ban trade tu tin hon. Moi giai doan phan anh hanh vi cua cac nhom trader khac nhau. Day la cuoc chien giua Buyer va Seller.

üß† Tam Ly Tung Giai Doan
  • Cup (Ben trai): Trader chot loi sau xu huong tang, gia giam dan
  • Day Cup: Nguoi mua moi bat dau tich luy, luc ban can kiet
  • Cup (Ben phai): Niem tin quay lai, gia tang dan len dinh cu
  • Handle: Lan chot loi cuoi cung truoc khi breakout manh
  • Breakout: Moi nguoi nhan ra xu huong tang tiep tuc
üí° Key Insight

Handle la co hoi cuoi cung de vao lenh truoc khi gia tang manh. Nhung trader chuyen nghiep thuong doi Handle hinh thanh moi trade. Ho khong trade khi gia dang o day Cup.

üìà Cac Bien The Cua Pattern

Ngoai Cup and Handle chuan, con co mot so bien the. Hieu cac bien the giup ban nhan dien pattern trong nhieu tinh huong. Moi bien the co dac diem rieng.

‚òï Cup Without Handle

Gia breakout ngay sau khi Cup hoan thanh. Pattern nay it chac chan hon nhung van co the trade.

üìâ High Handle

Handle hinh thanh o vi tri cao hon binh thuong. Thuong la dau hieu cua su manh me bat thuong.

üîÑ Double Cup

Hai Cup lien tiep truoc khi breakout. Pattern nay hiem gap nhung rat manh khi xuat hien.

‚¨áÔ∏è Inverted Cup and Handle

Phien ban dao nguoc, bao hieu xu huong giam. Thuong xuat hien o dinh thi truong.

‚úÖ Meo Chuyen Nghiep

Tap trung vao Cup and Handle chuan truoc. Khi da thanh thao, ban co the mo rong sang cac bien the. Dung co gang trade moi pattern ban thay.

üìã Tom Tat Bai Hoc
