üìö Rising Wedge La Gi?

Rising Wedge la mot trong nhung bearish reversal pattern manh nhat tren thi truong. Pattern nay xuat hien khi gia tang dan nhung dong luc yeu di. No bao hieu xu huong tang sap ket thuc va gia se giam manh.

Hinh dung ban dang leo doc mot ngon nui. Cang len cao, duong di cang hep va doc hon. Den mot luc, ban khong the di tiep va phai quay xuong. Rising Wedge cung hoat dong tuong tu nhu vay.

üéØ Dinh nghia chinh xac

Rising Wedge la pattern voi hai duong trendline hoi tu huong len. Duong resistance noi cac dinh cao dan. Duong support noi cac day cao dan. Khoang cach giua hai duong thu hep lai theo thoi gian.

Hinh 2.1.1: Cau truc co ban cua Rising Wedge - Bearish Reversal Pattern

üîß Cau Truc Cua Rising Wedge

De nhan dien Rising Wedge chinh xac, ban can hieu ro 4 thanh phan quan trong. Moi thanh phan deu co vai tro rieng trong viec xac nhan pattern.

üìà
1. Uptrend Ban Dau
Rising Wedge thuong hinh thanh sau mot giai doan tang gia. Day la dieu kien tien quyet de pattern co y nghia reversal. Khong co uptrend truoc do, pattern se mat di y nghia.
üìê
2. Hai Duong Trendline Hoi Tu
Duong tren noi cac dinh Higher High. Duong duoi noi cac day Higher Low. Ca hai duong deu huong len nhung duong duoi doc hon. Dieu nay tao hinh nem thu hep dan.
üìâ
3. Volume Giam Dan
Volume thuong giam dan khi pattern phat trien. Day la dau hieu cho thay luc mua dang yeu di. Nguoi mua mat dan hung thu va dong luc. Dieu nay rat quan trong de xac nhan pattern.
üí•
4. Breakdown
Gia pha vo duong support phia duoi voi volume tang manh. Day la tin hieu vao lenh Short. Breakdown thuong xay ra o khoang 2/3 chieu dai cua wedge.
üí° Luu Y Quan Trong

Rising Wedge can it nhat 2 diem cham cho moi duong trendline. Ly tuong la 3 diem cham tro len. Cang nhieu diem cham, pattern cang dang tin cay va win rate cang cao.

üß† Tam Ly Dang Sau Rising Wedge

Hieu tam ly thi truong giup ban trade pattern nay hieu qua hon. Moi giai doan cua Rising Wedge phan anh mot trang thai tam ly cu the cua nguoi choi.

üìà Giai Doan 1: Lac Quan

Nguoi mua van lac quan va day gia len cao. Ho tin rang xu huong tang se tiep tuc. Nhung moi dot tang gia lai yeu hon lan truoc.

üò∞ Giai Doan 2: Do Du

Nguoi mua bat dau do du khi gia tang cham lai. Ho van mua nhung khong con manh me nhu truoc. Volume giam dan la minh chung ro nhat.

üò® Giai Doan 3: Lo Lang

Nguoi mua lo lang khi thay gia bi nen trong khoang hep. Ho bat dau chot loi dan. Ap luc ban tang len trong khi luc mua yeu di.

üò± Giai Doan 4: Hoang Loan

Khi breakdown xay ra, nguoi mua hoang loan ban thao. Nguoi Short nhay vao. Gia giam manh va nhanh. Day la luc profit lon nhat.

üéØ Key Insight

Rising Wedge the hien su kiet suc cua phe mua. Gia tang nhung volume giam cho thay it nguoi muon mua o muc gia cao. Khi khong con ai mua, gia chi co the giam.

üìä Cach Xac Dinh Entry Va Target

Biet cach vao lenh va dat target la ky nang thuc chien quan trong. Day la phuong phap duoc cac trader chuyen nghiep su dung nhieu nhat.

üéØ Entry Point
  • Conservative: Cho gia dong cua duoi support + retest thanh cong
  • Aggressive: Vao lenh ngay khi gia break xuong duoi support
  • Confirmation: Volume phai tang it nhat 50% so voi trung binh
üìè Price Target
  • Target 1: Chieu cao cua wedge (tu dinh den day) tinh tu diem breakdown
  • Target 2: Day cua uptrend truoc do (neu co)
  • Target 3: Fibonacci 61.8% retracement cua uptrend truoc do
üõë Stop Loss
  • Vi tri: Dat stop loss o tren dinh cao nhat cua wedge
  • Buffer: Them 1-2% de tranh bi quet boi noise
  • Risk/Reward: Dam bao ty le toi thieu 1:2 truoc khi vao lenh

Hinh 2.1.2: Vi tri Entry, Target va Stop Loss cho Rising Wedge

‚ö†Ô∏è Nhung Loi Thuong Gap

Trader moi thuong mac nhung loi nay khi trade Rising Wedge. Hoc tu sai lam cua nguoi khac giup ban tranh mat tien khong dang co.

‚ùå Vao Lenh Som

Nhieu trader vao Short truoc khi breakdown xac nhan. Gia co the tiep tuc tang trong wedge. Cho confirmation la quy tac vang.

‚ùå Bo Qua Volume

Volume la yeu to xac nhan quan trong nhat. Breakdown khong co volume la tin hieu yeu. Luon kiem tra volume truoc khi vao lenh.

‚ùå Target Qua Tham

Dat target qua xa dan den miss profit. Chot loi dung muc quan trong. Dung tham lam qua muc can thiet.

‚ùå Nham Voi Channel

Rising Channel co hai duong song song. Rising Wedge co hai duong hoi tu. Su khac biet nay rat quan trong.

üìã Tom Tat Bai Hoc
  • Rising Wedge la bearish reversal pattern voi win rate 67-72%
  • Hai duong trendline hoi tu huong len, khoang cach thu hep dan
  • Volume giam dan la dau hieu xac nhan quan trong
  • Entry khi gia break xuong support voi volume tang
  • Target bang chieu cao wedge tinh tu diem breakdown
  • Stop loss dat tren dinh cao nhat cua wedge
