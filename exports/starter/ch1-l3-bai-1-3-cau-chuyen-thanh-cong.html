Hinh 3: Em Minh - Sinh vien tu lap tai chinh

üîó Diem Chung Cua Ho La Gi?

Ba nguoi - ba hoan canh khac nhau - nhung tat ca deu dat duoc ket qua. Hay xem ho co gi giong nhau:

üö´
Khong co background tai chinh
Khong ai co bang cap ve tai chinh, chung khoan, hay kinh te. Ho la nhung nguoi binh thuong, bat dau tu con so 0.
üí∞
Bat dau voi von nho
Khong ai bat dau voi hang tram trieu. Ho bat dau voi so tien minh co the mat duoc ma khong anh huong cuoc song.
üìö
Co HE THONG ro rang
Tat ca deu hoc va ap dung phuong phap GEM Frequency - mot he thong co quy tac cu the, khong phai "cam tinh" hay "du doan".
‚è∞
Kien tri va ky luat
Khong ai thanh cong sau 1 tuan hay 1 thang. Ho danh thoi gian 3-12 thang de hoc, thuc hanh, va ren luyen ky luat.

üí° Thong diep: Neu ho lam duoc, ban cung co the. Khong phai vi ho dac biet gi, ma vi ho da tim duoc he thong dung va kien tri ap dung no.

KIEM TRA KIEN THUC

1 Diem chung cua tat ca nhung nguoi thanh cong trong bai hoc la gi?
A Ho deu co background tai chinh hoac kinh te
B Ho bat dau voi so von lon hon nguoi binh thuong

Giai thich: Tat ca nhung nguoi thanh cong trong bai hoc deu co 4 diem chung: khong co background tai chinh, bat dau voi von nho, co HE THONG ro rang (GEM Frequency), va kien tri ap dung trong 6-12 thang.

A Kiem duoc nhieu tien trong thoi gian ngan
B Dat duoc cam giac tu do, an tam, va lam chu cuoc song
C Tro thanh trieu phu nhanh chong
D Nghi viec va lam trader toan thoi gian
Giai thich: Trong moi cau chuyen, ket qua quan trong nhat khong chi la tien ma con la CAM XUC dat duoc: an tam, tu do, can bang, tu hao, truong thanh. Day la nhung gia tri thuc su cua tu do tai chinh.
