üìö Tai Sao Can Ket Hop?

Trading chi dua vao pattern don le co Win Rate khoang 60-70%. Khi ket hop pattern voi GEM Zones, Win Rate co the tang len 75-85%. Day la bi mat cua cac trader Elite.

GEM Zones la cac vung gia quan trong ma GEM Scanner tu dong xac dinh. Chung bao gom Supply Zone, Demand Zone, va Key Level. Pattern xuat hien tai cac vung nay co do tin cay cao hon nhieu.

üìñ GEM Zones La Gi?

GEM Zones la cac vung gia co y nghia ky thuat quan trong. Supply Zone la vung ban manh. Demand Zone la vung mua manh. Key Level la muc gia tam ly quan trong. GEM Scanner tu dong phat hien cac vung nay.

üîß Cup and Handle + GEM Zones

Cup and Handle la pattern manh. Khi ket hop voi GEM Zones, no tro nen con manh hon. Duoi day la cac tinh huong ly tuong de trade.

Cup and Handle + Demand Zone

Day Cup hinh thanh ngay tai Demand Zone la tin hieu cuc manh. Demand Zone la vung Buyer san sang mua manh. Khi Cup bounce tu Demand Zone, kha nang breakout thanh cong rat cao.

Win Rate: 78-82%
‚úÖ Cach Nhan Dien

  • Day Cup cham hoac nam trong Demand Zone
  • Handle hinh thanh sau khi gia roi khoi Demand Zone
  • Breakout xay ra voi volume cao
üìê Ke Hoach Trade

  • Stop Loss: Duoi day Demand Zone (an toan hon day Cup)

Hinh 4.4.2: Rising Three Methods voi ba nen giua test Demand Zone

‚ö° Quy Tac Vang Khi Ket Hop

Ket hop pattern voi zones can tuan theo mot so quy tac. Nhung quy tac nay giup ban tranh cac setup xau. Hoc thuoc chung de trade an toan hon.

üìã 5 Quy Tac Vang
  • Quy tac 1: Chi trade khi pattern NAM TRONG hoac CHAM vao Zone. Pattern xa Zone khong tinh.
  • Quy tac 2: Zone phai CHUA bi test qua nhieu. Zone bi test 3+ lan tro nen yeu.
  • Quy tac 3: Volume xac nhan la bat buoc. Pattern + Zone + Volume = Setup hoan hao.
  • Quy tac 4: Tranh trade nguoc xu huong lon. Uptrend chi Long, Downtrend chi Short.
  • Quy tac 5: Doi pattern hoan chinh truoc khi vao lenh. Khong du doan, chi phan ung.

üéØ Confluence - Nhieu Yeu To Hoi Tu

Confluence la khi nhieu yeu to ky thuat dong thoi chi cung mot huong. Cang nhieu yeu to, trade cang chac chan. Day la cach trader Elite chon setup.

üéØ GEM Zone

Supply Zone, Demand Zone, Key Level. Pattern phai lien quan den Zone.

üìà Trend

üìâ Volume

üí° Key Insight

Setup co 4 yeu to confluence co Win Rate 80%+. Setup chi co 2 yeu to co Win Rate 60-65%. Luon tim setup voi it nhat 3 yeu to confluence.
